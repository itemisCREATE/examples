YAKINDU Labs Projects

Researching, prototyping, and putting our ideas into code, aside from the day-to-day business, is an essential part of itemis' company culture. YAKINDU Labs is a collection of projects that are currently in beta phase and therefore not yet released with any YAKINDU product bundles. As we love early feedback, we regularly blog about what's going on behind the scenes. So try it, and get in touch with us!
